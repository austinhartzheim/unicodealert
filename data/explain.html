Any Questions?

Why did I receive a warning?

You received this warning because you visited a website with a suspicious URL.

Websites that use foreign character sets can attempt to impersonate a legitimate website by using special letters to disguise the fact that you are visiting a similar website. For example, éxample.com looks very similar to example.com. One version has an accent mark over the letter "e."

Is my computer secure?

If this was a dangerous website, it is possible that malware was installed. Following the instructions from the non-profit Stop Badware organization will help reduce your risk, but cannot guarantee security.

If you believe you are at risk, you should have your computer examined by a professional.

How can I prevent these warnings?

Never click on links in emails or other messages that claim to be from a bank, financial institution, or government agency. Instead, attempt to navigate to the website directly or visit the office in person.
